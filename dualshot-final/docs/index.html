How it works

Record your video

Point, frame, and hit the red button. Front or back camera, with torch if you need it.

Preview both crops

See the 9:16 portrait and 16:9 landscape versions side-by-side before committing.

Save to gallery

Both videos land in your DualShot album. Upload the portrait to Reels, the landscape to YouTube.

Privacy

DualShot collects zero data. No analytics. No crash reporting. No network requests. The app works entirely offline. Your videos are processed on-device and saved to your local gallery. We never see your footage.
